Self-Care

Self-Care during periods should emphasize both physical and emotional well-being, focusing on hygiene, comfort, and relaxation.

Physical Self-Care

1. Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to help reduce bloating and alleviate cramps.

2. Warm Compresses: Apply warm compresses to your lower abdomen to help relieve cramps.

3. Exercise: Engage in gentle exercises like yoga,walking or swimming to help reduce symptoms.


Emotional Self-Care

1. Practice Relaxation Techniques: Try deep breathing,meditation,or progressive muscle relaxationto help manage stress and anxiety.

2. Engage in activites you enjoy: Do things that bring you joy and help take your mind off symptoms.

3. Prioritize self-compassion: Be kind and gentle with yourself, and avoid self-criticism.
